Null Horizon: Plasma Burn

Plasma Burn is a table top wargame set in the Null Horizon universe. I have been developing it since 2020 with my friends, Michael Laba and Shane Sabaj.

After the accidental activation of a super weapon constructed by an alien precursor race, the Terran Coalition, who are the institutional government of humanity, is split into two segments. by a galaxy-spanning storm. It is not until the Coalition develops the technology to create portals from the storm itself to finally travel to the other side. And in the century between the storm's arrival and finally being able to cross the Rift that the Coalition finds new factions on the other side occupying the systems and planets they seek to reclaim, thus sparking the Dark Sector Conflicts.
