Null Horizon: Plasma Burn

Plasma Burn is a table top wargame set in the Null Horizon universe. I have been developing it since 2020 with my friends, Michael Laba and Shane Sabaj.

It's set during the Dark Sector conflicts, following the Milky Way Galaxy being split in half from the activation of an alien artifact.

SCAR Troopers are cyborg commandos of the Terran Coalition in Null Horizon. They were created after Terran troops observed the cyborg super soldiers of the Novite Brotherhood, the Paragons, and how deadly they were in close quarters.
